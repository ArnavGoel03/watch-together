## Name
Watch Together - Sync Videos with Friends

## Short Description (132 char max)
Watch Netflix, YouTube, JioHotstar & any video together in real-time with friends. Sync playback, chat & share the experience!

## Detailed Description

Watch Together lets you sync video playback across any number of devices worldwide. Watch movies, shows, and videos with friends and family — no matter where they are.

WORKS WITH EVERY VIDEO SITE:
- YouTube
- Netflix
- JioHotstar (Hotstar)
- Disney+ Hotstar
- Amazon Prime Video
- HBO Max
- Hulu
- Twitch
- And any website with a video player!

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Install Watch Together on Chrome, Edge, or Brave
2. Click the extension icon and create a room
3. Share the room code with friends
4. Everyone navigates to the same video
5. Play, pause, or seek — it syncs for everyone instantly!

KEY FEATURES:
- Real-time sync — play, pause, seek syncs across all devices
- Equal control — everyone can control playback, not just the host
- Built-in chat — talk with friends while watching
- Room codes — easy 6-character codes to join
- Shareable links — one-click copy to share with friends
- Auto drift correction — stays perfectly in sync even on slow connections
- Works on any video site — universal HTML5 video support
- Dark theme — beautiful UI that doesn't distract from your content
- No account required — just enter your name and go
- Lightweight — tiny footprint, no impact on video quality

PERFECT FOR:
- Long-distance couples watching movies together
- Friends streaming shows across different cities
- Study groups watching educational content
- Family movie nights when you're apart
- Watch parties for premieres and live events

PRIVACY:
- No data collection
- No tracking
- No account required
- Room data is temporary and deleted when everyone leaves
- Open source
